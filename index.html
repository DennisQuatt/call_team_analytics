Sales Support methodology: Calls from FCT_CALLS (Aircall, all directions). Unanswered = ANSWERED_AT IS NULL. Meetings = deals entering DATE_ENTERED_WPS7 attributed to QUALIFICATION_CALL_OWNER (i.e. the support agent who handled the qualification call that led to the meeting). HVs = deals entering DATE_ENTERED_WPS6HV with the same qualification-owner attribution. All figures are activity-only (no pipeline/revenue metrics for this team).
